How come?

That we love cats so much? It'a a story too long to be told with one website. We will only point out the most important things.

Our Mission

From 2015, our founders, Alex, Jamie and Cian share the same goal and are dedicated to help any cat in need. It is estimated that there is in excess of 200,000 feral cat colonies across Ireland.

>

Our vibe

At CatFinder, we do miracles. We dream and idealise, and then do it-together. We push to our limits to try to reinvent what's possible, all for better life for our cats.

Our promise

We are a fair policy charity and we welcome everyone. We deliver the best possible care to our temporary residents and foster them with great care. All of the cats are treated with love and respect they deserve.

We provide:

  • Vaccinations for all our cats on premises and post-fostering care
  • Vet care and yearly checkups for all of our clients
  • Micro chipping
  • Access to any cats records that your need
  • Spraying or Neutering
  • Catsitters
